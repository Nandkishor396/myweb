mentoring India

Mentoring India is our project name, and we are working on a Mission for quality and affordable Education, Skill Development, Entrepreneurship, Career Guidance & Mentoring. Under this program, students (mentees) will undertake a career exploration journey that will help them make informed career choices.

Features :

1. Mentorship Program for K-12

2. Mentorship Program for UPSC | BPSC
3. Entrepreneurship
4. Career Counseling
5. Mentoring
6. Skill Development
7. Personalised Coaching & Training



Our Beliefs

We believe in a world where every Youth can read and choose better Career Options. Our mission is to invest in young minds and early childhood education in order to empower the next generation. We do that, by creating educational programs and providing necessary resources in underprivileged areas.
Our Vision
  • To empower Youth and convert their hidden Potential into Entrepreneurship.
  • Building a community of youth and working to create spaces for students/ Youth to have an open dialogue about their careers, opinions and everyday choices.
Our Mission
  • To make students aware of the diverse set of careers to choose from.
  • To guide and support students in their initial career planning stages.
  • To develop the aptitude to handle personal and professional challenges.
  • To enhance philanthropic outlook in the community of youth for nation-building.
